Style:
- editorial
- minimal
- premium
- calm
- cinematic — not clinical stock

Avoid:
- generic medical UI
- stock-looking layouts
- heavy gradients
- neon / startup gradients
- overuse of icons
- repeated card-grid patterns

Typography:
- strong hierarchy
- large, confident headings
- minimal body text
- avoid clutter
- generous spacing

Color:
- dark / neutral tones preferred
- limited palette
- no overuse of accent colors

Imagery:
- real environment preferred
- no cliché stock photos
- avoid overly staged visuals
- soft clinical lighting — human, not cold

Layout:
- strong above-the-fold
- clear entry points
- no clutter
- no card overload
- prefer breathing layouts
- asymmetrical where it serves the content

Design:
- use parallax effect
- negative space is intentional, not empty
- services/procedures feel curated, not listed for sale
