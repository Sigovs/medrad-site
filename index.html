Featured Procedures

What we treat

Minimally invasive, image-guided procedures for conditions that were once managed only with open surgery. Shorter recovery. Fewer risks. The same clinical outcomes.

Embolotherapy

Uterine Fibroid Embolization

A non-surgical treatment for symptomatic fibroids. UFE shrinks fibroids by blocking their blood supply — preserving the uterus and avoiding general anaesthesia.

Learn more →

Neurointerventions

Vertebroplasty & Kyphoplasty

Percutaneous stabilisation of vertebral compression fractures using bone cement. Rapid pain relief — a same-day, outpatient procedure with immediate weight-bearing.

Learn more →

Venous Interventions

Hemodialysis Access

Creation, maintenance, and salvage of vascular access for patients requiring long-term hemodialysis. Includes AV fistula and AV graft management.

Learn more →

Arterial Interventions

Peripheral Arterial Disease (PAD)

Balloon angioplasty and stenting to restore circulation in limbs affected by arterial narrowing or occlusion — reducing claudication and preventing limb loss.

Learn more →
