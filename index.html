What we treat

Minimally invasive, image-guided procedures for conditions that were once managed only with open surgery. Shorter recovery. Fewer risks. The same clinical outcomes.

Signature Procedure · Embolotherapy

Uterine Fibroid Embolization

Heavy periods. Pelvic pain. Without surgery. UFE shrinks fibroids by blocking their blood supply — preserving the uterus and avoiding general anaesthesia.

  • Time~45 min
  • SettingOutpatient
  • Recovery~2 days
